About Yuvaan Dilicious Foods


Founded by Ms Neelam Shakya & Mr Nirbhya Kushvaha

Welcome to Yuvaan Dilicious Foods, a haven for vegetarians seeking an authentic and delectable Chinese culinary experience in the heart of Delhi. Founded by the visionary Neelam Shakya, our establishment is a celebration of flavors, innovation, and a commitment to serving exceptional vegetarian Chinese cuisine. At Yuvaan Dilicious Foods, we believe in the power of plant-based ingredients to create dishes that not only tantalize your taste buds but also contribute to a healthier lifestyle. Neelam Shakya's passion for vegetarianism and Chinese cuisine has inspired the creation of a menu that showcases the rich diversity of flavors, textures, and aromas that can be achieved without the use of meat. Our chefs are dedicated to crafting dishes that reflect the authenticity of Chinese culinary traditions while infusing a modern twist. From savory dumplings to flavorful stir-fries, each dish is a masterpiece that showcases the harmonious blend of fresh, locally sourced vegetables and expertly chosen spices. We take pride in our commitment to quality, and every ingredient used in our kitchen undergoes rigorous scrutiny to ensure that it meets our high standards. The Food Shop Open is not just a restaurant; it's a reflection of our dedication to providing you with an unforgettable dining experience. Beyond the delicious food, our welcoming ambiance and attentive staff aim to make every visit a memorable one. We invite you to savor the unique flavors of our vegetarian Chinese dishes, prepared with love and care, and discover a culinary journey that will leave you coming back for more. Thank you for choosing Yuvaan Dilicious Foods. We look forward to serving you and being a part of your delightful moments in the world of vegetarian Chinese cuisine.
